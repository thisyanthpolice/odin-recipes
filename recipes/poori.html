Poori Recipe

Description:

Poori is a deep-fried Indian bread made with whole wheat flour. It is soft, puffed, and often served with potato curry or chole.

Ingredients:

  • Whole Wheat Flour - 1 cup
  • Water - As needed
  • Salt - To taste
  • Oil - For frying

Steps:

  1. Mix wheat flour, salt, and water to form a stiff dough. Knead well.
  2. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into small discs.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan.
  4. Deep-fry each poori until it puffs up and turns golden brown.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with potato curry or chole.
