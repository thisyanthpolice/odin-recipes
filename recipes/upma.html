Upma Recipe

Description:

Upma is a savory South Indian breakfast dish made with semolina (rava). It is cooked with vegetables and seasoned with spices for a wholesome meal.

Ingredients:

  • Semolina (Rava) - 1 cup
  • Onions - 1 (finely chopped)
  • Green Chilies - 2 (slit)
  • Mustard Seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Curry Leaves - A few
  • Water - 2 cups
  • Salt - To taste
  • Oil or Ghee - 2 tablespoons

Steps:

  1. Dry roast the semolina in a pan until aromatic. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add mustard seeds.
  3. Once they splutter, add curry leaves, green chilies, and onions. Saut√© until onions are soft.
  4. Add water and salt. Bring to a boil.
  5. Gradually add the roasted semolina while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  6. Cook until the upma thickens and is well-cooked.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh coriander or grated coconut.
