Vada Recipe

Description:

Vada is a crispy and savory South Indian fritter made with urad dal batter. It is deep-fried to golden perfection and often enjoyed with chutneys and sambar.

Ingredients:

  • Urad Dal (Black Gram Lentils) - 1 cup
  • Green Chilies - 2 (finely chopped)
  • Onions - 1 (finely chopped)
  • Salt - To taste
  • Oil - For frying

Steps:

  1. Soak urad dal in water for 4-6 hours or overnight.
  2. Grind the dal into a thick, smooth batter.
  3. Add chopped green chilies, onions, and salt to the batter.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan.
  5. Shape the batter into small round discs and deep-fry until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with chutneys and sambar.
